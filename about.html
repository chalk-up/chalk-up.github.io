About

Chalk Up is a fun project to source the best climbing knowledge on the web and put it all in one place! We will cover everything from technique and rehab to mental strength and climbing films so you can spend more time living the climbing-lifestyle and less time trying to find the right information.

As climbers ourselves, we have spent an awful lot of time trying to put together our own training plans, learn about injuries and watch awesome climbing videos. We have found resources in the most bizarre, hidden and unfindable places on the web and some of it is absolute gold-dust.

We hope you enjoy using Chalk Up and can use the resources to help you hit your climbing goals. If we are missing anything or you want to see a topic covered, give us a shout!
